Description

This is a dish that originated from italy. It consits of tomato sauce on bread with metled cheese on top. It is commonly served with toppings

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • toppings

Recipe

  1. Create a circular disk of pizza dough
  2. Spread sauce evenly across the pizza dough
  3. Sprinkle cheese evenly across the pizza dough
  4. add toppings to the pizza
  5. Put in oven for enough time to cook it
  6. Use pizza cutter to make slices and enjoy
