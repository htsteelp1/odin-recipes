Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwhich

Home

Description

Tasty, healthy, and delicious mix of peanut butter and jelly in the formfactor of a sandwich. You would not expect this pairing to have such great success, but the crunchiness of the peanut butter and the sweetness of the jelly create a delicious pair!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Recipe

  1. Spread Peanut Butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on another slice of bread
  3. Put the two halves together and enjoy!
